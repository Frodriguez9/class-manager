This is a backend API that will slowly undergo a metamorphosis until it becomes a fully functional SPA. using the Vue.js framework.

The goal is to allows curious minds and talented people - who are willing to teach their skills using a webcam, to come together. The core idea is that instructors create classes and students enroll in them, allowing instructors to manage their online classes from here. That is: student registration, payments, and meeting-link-sharing automation.
